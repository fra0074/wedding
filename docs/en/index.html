Getting There

Brindisi Airport (BDS)

From Brindisi Airport (45 km): Closest option. Car rental recommended. Travel time: 45 minutes.

Bari Airport (BRI)

From Bari Airport (80 km): Rent a car or take a bus to Francavilla Fontana. Travel time: approximately 1 hour.

From Brindisi

From Brindisi: Follow SS7 towards Francavilla Fontana. Direct route of 45 km.

From Bari

From Bari: Follow A14 towards Brindisi, then SS7 towards Francavilla Fontana.

By Train

From Rome/Milan: Train to Bari or Brindisi, then local bus or car rental to reach the venue.
